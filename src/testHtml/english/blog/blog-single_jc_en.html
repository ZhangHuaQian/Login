From 2010 to 2014, 2,129 policemen were killed in the line of duty, among which 111, or 5.2 percent, were killed fighting against criminals (who suffered violent attacks). On average, 28 policemen were killed every year for catching criminals.In terms of age, the average number of civilian police killed in the line of duty was about 45.5 years old, and the proportion of young and middle-aged police killed was relatively high, among which 70% were aged from 30 to 49, 10% were under 29 and 20% were aged over 50.

In terms of the causes of sacrifice, sudden death due to sudden illness accounted for more than half of the total number on the job, which was the primary cause of civilian police sacrifice on duty.Second, traffic accidents caused police sacrifice on duty accounted for 26.3 percent.A total of 111 people, or 5.2%, were killed fighting against criminals (victims of violent attacks). Except for a decrease in 2014, the number and proportion of those killed in the first four years increased continuously.Rescue and relief sacrifice accounted for 3.7%.From the police, the police station, traffic patrol, public security police, criminal investigation police, fire officers and men sacrifice in the top five.
Xinjiang, which is on the front line of maintaining stability against terrorism, ranks first in China in terms of police sacrifice and injuries.Zhang chunxian, former party secretary of xinjiang uygur autonomous region, revealed that the sacrifice rate of police officers in xinjiang is 5.4 times that of the national police officers.On the road to terrorism, is the smoke filled battlefield, is the forefront of the hail of bullets, how many heroes blood sprinkled battlefield, heroic dedication.One poem said: "a thousand miles of road, turn leisurely, and cross the sea blue clouds sky.Regardless of life and death, more or less success in the smile.Is not the true portrayal of the police!
