Nan rendong, the father of China's "heavenly eye"

Since 1994, I have been responsible for the site selection, pre-study, project approval, feasibility study and preliminary design of FAST. As the chief scientist and chief engineer of the project, I was responsible for compiling FAST scientific goals, comprehensively guiding the construction of FAST project, and taking charge of overcoming a series of technical problems such as cable fatigue and moving optical cable. On September 25, 2016, FAST was inaugurated.

In 2014, the "heavenly eye" reflector unit will be hoisted, and nan rendong himself will conduct the "small flying man" manned test. Before the opening of the "China eye" in September 2016, nan rendong was already suffering from lung cancer and injured vocal cords during surgery. After illness still insist on work, although the body is not suitable for the boat car tired, still fly to guizhou from Beijing, witnessed the completion of the big scientific project that spent 22 years of effort.
