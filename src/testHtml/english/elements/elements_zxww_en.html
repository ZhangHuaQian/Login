Core and Periphery Theory

Let's start with the center-periphery theory, which is a model that was developed after world war ii by raul prevish, an Argentine economist, who noticed that after world war ii, a lot of Asia and Africa Latin American countries have successively got rid of the colonial rule of western countries and achieved political independence, but they have not achieved economic independence or modernization or national wealth and strong people The reason is that the world is made up of two parts, the central countries, which are the western countries, and the peripheral countries, which are the developing countries, The central country is in the absolute dominant position, the developing countries are attached to the central country and oppressed and exploited by the central country, they can only be engaged in the production of raw materials or Production of primary materials. In such a system, developing countries are unlikely to develop. In qiao guanhua's words, such dependence is more like riding on A rider on a horse. Western imperialist countries, however, are making every effort to draw China into the periphery of this "central peripheral system".
