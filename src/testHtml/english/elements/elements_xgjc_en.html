Tribute to Hong Kong police

Recently, a series of illegal and violent incidents have taken place in Hong Kong, seriously undermining public order and stability. Hong Kong police enforce the law and fight crime in a civilized manner. They handle the situation with restraint. They are supposed to maintain order in line with their duty, but they are distorted by the western media into "tyranny" and "excessive use of force". Some foreign media are constantly pointing fingers, making double standards and criticizing the normal law enforcement actions of the Hong Kong police.

首页 Tribute to Hong Kong police

cause

Hong Kong's business and legal communities have objected to the start of the amendment process on February 13. The business community believes that this may affect the business environment in Hong Kong, while the legal community believes that the amendment will undermine the independence of the judiciary in Hong Kong. Violence in Hong Kong has escalated since June. On June 9th a big demonstration broke out against the ban. On June 21, demonstrators surrounded the police headquarters and blocked the entrance and exit, set up barricades with iron horses and water horses, blocked the main road nearby, threw eggs at the police headquarters, and shone lasers at police officers. On June 26, demonstrators surrounded the police headquarters and removed a plaque that read "Hong Kong police headquarters."

Time goes by, but time will see and record many things. In the past two months, we have heard with great sadness that Hong Kong, a stable and prosperous city, is suffering from the deliberate violence of some radical violators and the opposition Events, completely beyond the scope of peaceful demonstrations, out of the track of demand. As the guardian of social order, the Hong Kong police acted as the guardian of social order during the whole spreading process of the incident Under great pressure, the Hong Kong police are still bearing their mission in mind.

For nearly two months, militant violence has defied the rule of law by attacking with bricks, iron branches, umbrellas and even gasoline Police, use poisonous and harmful liquid, powder attack police act cruel. Laser guns were used to target police and citizens and to destroy the national emblem and flag. Barricade up Traffic, vandalism, resulting in vehicles and subway stations can not run. In the harsh environment, the Hong Kong police have endured the humiliation, performed their duties and showed no fear. Maintain social stability, get the support and support of the general public.

Social stability and tranquility have not come easily. We have been resolute in saying no to radical and violent elements and to those who seek to destabilize Hong Kong by destroying the "one country, two systems" principle. Stop violence, curb violence and guard Hong Kong. To pay high tribute to the obscure efforts of the police, support the Hong Kong police and guard Hong Kong.
