Police said it was common for pickpockets to try to evade punishment by suffering from serious infectious diseases during anti-pickpocketing duties, and some police officers were even bitten by suspects. On December 23, 2013, the national health and family planning commission (NHFPC) jointly issued the revised occupational disease classification and catalogue, which adjusted 132 occupational diseases and increased 18 occupational diseases. Among them, the people's police in the execution of official duties or occupational activities infected with AIDS will be included in the scope of occupational infectious diseases, can enjoy treatment such as injury insurance.
