Tribute to tiangong I

Seven years of life, if viewed from a human perspective, tiangong I is still a child. However, as the country's first target aircraft and space laboratory, it officially entered service for two years, and then flew in space to today.On April 2, 2018, tiangong 1 will re-enter mother earth, burning up in the atmosphere and dying, with only the debris scattered to the earth.China's next-generation space station is set to be completed, while its successor, tiangong-2, remains aloft hundreds of kilometres.

——The dragon rises into the sky to greet "China palace"
At 21:16 am on September 29, 2011, a moment destined to be enshrined in China's space history, tiangong-1 was lifted up by a long March 2F rocket and flew to a vast space more than 300 kilometers away.This day, the vast sky ushered in a "China palace."Tiangong-1 is 10.4 meters long with a maximum diameter of 3.35 meters and consists of a laboratory module and a resource module.Its launch marks the second phase of China's three-step space strategy.

——Kiss in space with shenzhou 8 spacecraft
After 32 days in the air and 485 solo dances around the earth, tiangong-1 was lowered into a rendezvous and docking orbit 343 kilometers above the earth.At 1:36 am on November 3, 2011, the tiangong-1 target spacecraft successfully docked with the shenzhou viii spacecraft for the first time, which is known as the beautiful "space kiss".This is the first space rendezvous and docking test completed by China's manned space program.After 12 days of dancing around the earth, tiangong-1 and shenzhou-8 separated briefly.At 20 o 'clock on the 14th, the two partners held hands again and achieved the second rendezvous and docking successfully.Three days later, tiangong-1 waved goodbye to the shenzhou-8 spacecraft and returned to long-term orbit from rendezvous and docking.

——China's first manned docking
At around 14:00 on June 18, 2012, after completing the procedures of capturing, cushioning, narrowing and locking, shenzhou-9 and tiangong-1 were closely linked, and China's first manned rendezvous and docking was successful.Chinese astronauts jing haipeng, liu wang and liu Yang entered tiangong-1 at 17:04 am on Sunday, giving the space station a real "home of China". Liu wang, who operated the shenzhou-9 spacecraft and tiangong-1 spacecraft, successfully completed the manual docking Thursday, winning world acclaim with a Chinese accuracy of less than 7 minutes and 18 millimeters by mistake.This is China's first successful manned docking.28, in the combination of the body resides in 10 days of astronauts bid farewell to tiangong I.

——China's first space lesson

At 13:18 on June 11, 2013, the shenzhou-10 spacecraft carrying three astronauts successfully achieved automatic rendezvous and docking with the tiangong-1 target spacecraft.This is the fifth time tiangong-1 has successfully docked with a shenzhou spacecraft since it was launched into orbit in September 2011.China's first "space lecture" was successfully held in space Tuesday morning.This is the highest platform in China - more than 300 kilometers away from the ground, tiangong-1, shenzhou-10 astronauts nie haisheng, zhang xiaoguang and wang yaping give the nation's teenagers a magical lesson in space.This is the largest classroom in China -- from the capital Beijing to the motherland, more than 80,000 middle schools and tens of millions of teachers and students listen to the astronauts' lectures in space through radio, TV and Internet live broadcasts, and enjoy the wonderful space world together.

Curtain call, hard to say goodbye

On March 16, 2016, the tiangong-1 target spacecraft officially terminated its data service, completing its historical mission and entering the period of orbital decay.From the launch on September 29, 2011, during the tiangong 1 on-orbit successively with the shenzhou 8 manned spacecraft, shenzhou 9 and shenzhou 10 into the six rendezvous and docking, including four times and automatic rendezvous and docking two hand control rendezvous and docking, and verify the several key technical station, China became the world the third independence to master the rendezvous and docking technology.Dribs and drabs, the sky for evidence, stars for evidence.Thanks to tiangong-1's pioneering contribution, China will be able to build a large, long-term national space laboratory around 2020.Tiangong I left a lot of beauty and shock in China and around the world.It's hard to say goodbye to the stars.Tiangong 1 is far away, but tiangong 2 is still making great achievements.In the vast universe, China's spaceflight has never stopped.
