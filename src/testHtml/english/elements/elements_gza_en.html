Hong Kong-Zhuhai-Macao Bridge

Hong kong-zhuhai-macao bridge is a bridge and tunnel project connecting Hong Kong, zhuhai, guangdong and Macao within the waters of lingdingyang area of pearl river estuary, guangdong province, China.

home page Hong Kong-Zhuhai-Macao Bridge

Hats off to the hong kong-zhuhai-macao bridge team

Construction of the hong kong-zhuhai-macao bridge commenced on December 15, 2009; Completion of the main project on July 7, 2017; Completed the main project acceptance on February 6, 2018; It began operation at 9:00 am on October 24, 2018. Hong kong-zhuhai-macao bridge starts from the artificial island of Hong Kong port near Hong Kong international airport in the east, and connects zhuhai and Macao man-industrial island after the lingdingyang of the south China sea in the west, ending at the zhuhai hongwan interchange. The total length of the bridge and tunnel is 55 km, of which 29.6 km is the main bridge and 41.6 km is from the Hong Kong port to the zhuhai-macao port. The bridge deck is a two-way six-lane expressway with a designed speed of 100 km/h; [1] the total investment of the project is 126.9 billion yuan. From December 1, 2018, the first batch of guangdong-macao non-operating cars will be allowed to pass the cross-boundary section of the hong kong-zhuhai-macao bridge without visa addition. Hong kong-zhuhai-macao bridge is famous for its huge construction scale, unprecedented construction difficulty and top construction technology. The chief designer of the bridge project is meng fanchao, the chief engineer is su quanke, and the general manager and chief engineer of the island tunnel project is Lin Ming.
