FAST

Five-hundred-meter Aperture Spherical Telescope(FAST)，Located in the karst depression pit of dawodang, kedu town, pingtang county, guizhou province, buyi and miao autonomous prefecture, south guizhou province, the project is a national major science and technology infrastructure. The "tianeye" project consists of active reflector system, feed support system, measurement and control system, receiver and terminal and observation base.

Reminiscing about the father of "FAST

September 25, 2016, is important for FAST.It was the end of a 22-year scientific project.As the world's most The construction of FAST, a large single-aperture radio telescope, opens an "eye of heaven" for Chinese scientists to explore the unknown universe and the origin of life China's astronomical research is pushing into a wider and deeper world.

Over the years, FAST has been seen by millions of people in the media.In guizhou, even people who have nothing to do with it can talk to outsiders about the cauldron in the mountains.Yet little is known about the people behind this great project.In this group of people there are communist party members, there are ordinary people, there are well-known scholars, there are "green pepper".For them, the brief joy of completion is followed by a longer period of persistence and watch.

Without nan rendong, it is hard to imagine the "heavenly eye" standing in the world.For more than 20 years, from FAST's site selection, project approval and feasibility study, to guiding the research of various key technologies and model experiments, nan rendong seems to have been fascinated by this "heavenly eye" and devoted the rest of his life to it.The story starts in 1993.That year, the international radio science union congress was held in Tokyo, Japan.Scientists are proposing to build the next generation of radio telescopes, setting the stage for radio astronomy in 10 or 20 years."Let's build one too."Nan rendong, then deputy director of Beijing observatory of Chinese academy of sciences, and several colleagues proposed to build our own large telescope according to China's national conditions.From such a word to start, the south rendong himself and the "eye of heaven" firmly tied together. "These twenty years, south teacher did nothing else"Nan rendong's student gan hengqian said, these years, south rendong's efforts to the ordinary people can hardly imagine.In the world, it is the limit of the engineering to use the radio telescope with steel structure to exceed 100 meters.To build a larger aperture telescope, you need to choose a large, round hole and use the earth potential to do so.Guizhou's karst landscape, with its numerous potholes, is a natural candidate. In order to find the most satisfactory location, nan rendong selected all the depressions close to the circle from hundreds of remote sensing geological images, and went into the mountains of guizhou.He would climb over the mountains with a bamboo pole and survey the site to see if the depression was suitable and how far it was from the noisy market.

"In those years, Mr. Nan almost went to all the depressions in the area."Gan hengqian said, nan rendong climb the mountain road even the farmers there shook their heads.At that time, nan rendong's physical strength was outstanding among his peers.Picky south of the east finally phase of the most circular pit - located in guizhou pingtang county dawang.He then formally proposed the idea of using karst terrain to build large radio telescopes.In order to promote the project approval, every time nan rendong reports the project to relevant departments, he arrives at the meeting place at least one hour in advance.He was worried about being late because of a slight accident. He often needed to write a project introduction of three or five thousand words.Nan rendong and colleagues together in the office, word for word, often get into the wee hours.He was afraid that a slight omission would affect the success or failure of the project.

22 years of trials and hardships, the achievement of "FAST", also changed the nanrendong

Now, he had a sonorous voice, clearly feel the lack of breath, speak can only be broken to jump out, the legs and feet over the countless mountains began to become shambling and slow.But as long as the mention of FAST, south rendong is still hot."The next step is to make good use of this great tool of science and make it productive and give back to the country and the public and welcome more people to the country."”
