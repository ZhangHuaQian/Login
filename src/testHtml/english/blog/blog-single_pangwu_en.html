“CZ-5”do not cry

China's long March 5 rocket mission failed at 19:23 on July 2, 2017 at the wenchang space launch site. Subsequently, experts will be organized to investigate and analyze the cause of failure.

Originally, the launch meant the long March 5 carrier rocket project had come to a successful end and had entered the formal application stage.The launch is also China's last practical exercise for the "fat five" rocket ahead of the launch of the chang 'e-5 lunar probe, the third lunar probe in the second half of this year.Most of the previous Chinese liquid launch vehicles had a diameter of 2.25 meters or 3.35 meters, while the long March 5 rocket had a diameter of 5 meters.Compared with the "slim" rockets of the past, the long March 5 has been nicknamed "fat five".

The "fat five" is a new generation of carrier rocket with the most complicated technology and the largest carrying capacity in China.It was developed by the first institute of the China aerospace science and technology corporation (casc), realizing the leap of the diameter of China's liquid carrier rocket from 3.35 meters to 5 meters, marking the upgrade of China's carrier rocket.As one of the larger of China's long march rockets, the pangwu has the capability to carry 25 tons in low earth orbit and 14 tons in geosynchronous transfer orbit.The 7-ton shijian-18 satellite is currently the heaviest satellite launched in geosynchronous orbit developed by China. It will use laser communication and new hall electric propulsion systems to test and verify key technologies of dongfanghong 5 satellite platform in orbit.

Tribute to the long March 5 carrier rocket power development and test team

Who can imagine that they are a group of optimistic and open-minded, patient and determined, happy people, long years in extremely difficult conditions and extremely hot and cold products, the environment, never say a bitter and tired, in the "ice" "fire" in two days to seek another kind of happiness of life. They cried as the long March 5 soared to victory in its first battle.

The long March 5 is known as the "ice" arrow because its propellant is cryogenic.In which the temperature of the liquid hydrogen and liquid oxygen, respectively is - 252 ℃ and 183 ℃, 101 is not only in establishing the first low temperature technology research institute, is one of the biggest liquid hydrogen production units of production capacity in China at present, range refers to the set of liquid hydrogen supply unit, using their own technology advantage at the same time, actively carry out cryogenic valves, piping, and the design of the container production, as well as low temperature technology work programs application project development, etc.

A large amount of liquid hydrogen produced by 101 is used in rocket research and test and range flight and launch, and the low-temperature valve and vacuum adiabatic transport pipeline developed are widely used in space ground research and test equipment, hainan wenchang space launch site and so on.After years of dealing with these low-temperature media and products, they have developed a calm , rigorous and meticulous work style and habits.In particular, the storage tank of the long March 5 is the largest ever filled with liquid hydrogen and oxygen, requiring extremely special requirements on pipelines, equipment, filling procedures and safety measures.

The liquid hydrogen system of new-generation carrier rocket power system test bed is the system with the most complicated scale, the greatest technical difficulty, the most difficult quality control, the highest technical safety risk and the longest production and installation period among all the subsystems of power system test.For this purpose, 101 institute has added 2 liquid hydrogen storage tanks of 170 cubic meters, designed and constructed DN150 vacuum multilayer insulation pipeline with a length of more than 800 meters, which is the first in China in terms of storage capacity, length and caliber of pipeline.

In the design and construction of the test bed of the 101 rocket power system and the completion of the test bed of the new-generation carrier rocket power system, more than 10 key technologies, including the overall design technology of the test bed of the power system, were successfully broken through.Design of construction of the long March 5 test command management information system is the first ever by 101, is China's rocket developed ground test areas with the most advanced technology and the highest level of integration, the function most complete large visualization system, it can be said that the long sign 5 test information management system as a command by inserting "smart chip" super brain, the whole test process, the system won the aerospace science and technology group co., LTD is the title of scientific research and production of digital benchmarking project.
