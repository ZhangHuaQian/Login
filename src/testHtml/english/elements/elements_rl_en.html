The journey of a community of Shared future for mankind

Put forward the stage

The amendment to the constitution adopted at the first session of the 13th National People's Congress on March 11, 2018, reads in the twelfth preambular paragraph of the constitution: To develop diplomatic relations and economic and cultural exchanges with other countries A community of Shared future for mankind. On December 3, 2018, the community of Shared future was named one of the top 10 buzzwords of 2018.

sustainable development

Xi jinping put forward the initiative of "building a community of Shared future for mankind by moving toward a community of Shared future for Asia" when attending the 2015 annual conference of BBS in boao. He put forward the four principles for building a community of common destiny: all countries should respect and treat each other as equals, pursue win-win cooperation and common development, and pursue common development. Comprehensive, cooperative and sustainable security, featuring inclusiveness and mutual learning among different civilizations.

achievement
